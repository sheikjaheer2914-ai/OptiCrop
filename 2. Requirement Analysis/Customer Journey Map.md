# Customer Journey Map: Farmer Optimizing Crop Production

## 1. Persona Profile
- **User:** Rajesh Kumar (Small-scale Farmer)
- **Goal:** Identify the most profitable, high-yield crop for the upcoming monsoon season based on soil conditions.

## 2. Journey Stages

| Stage | 1. Awareness | 2. Data Collection | 3. App Interaction | 4. Implementation & Harvest |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Actions** | Notices declining seasonal crop yields and rising fertilizer costs. | Visits local lab to test soil or uses on-field sensor metrics. | Inputs soil parameters (N,P,K,pH) and location into OptiCrop App. | Plants the recommended crop and follows nutrient guidelines. |
| **Emotions** | Frustrated, Anxious | Overwhelmed | Relieved, Confident | Satisfied, Successful |
| **Pain Points** | Lack of clear analytical guidance. | Manual records are easy to lose or misinterpret. | Worries if the app supports his specific regional language. | Needs real-time disease alerts during rainy spells. |
