# Target User Empathy Map: The Modern Farmer

## 1. SAY (What the user explicitly states)
- "I want to know exactly what crop will give me the highest profit this season."
- "Fertilizers are getting expensive; I cannot afford to waste them on the wrong patches."

## 2. THINK (What goes on in the user's mind)
- Will a bad harvest ruin my family's financial stability this year?
- Is my soil permanently losing its health because of the chemicals I use?

## 3. DO (The actions the user currently takes)
- Manually checks the texture and moisture of the topsoil.
- Tracks local television news or radio broadcasts for basic rainfall predictions.

## 4. FEEL (The emotional state of the user)
- **Anxiety:** Constant worry over unpredicted droughts or low market yields.
- **Frustration:** Spending high amounts on seeds and labor only to get low outputs.
